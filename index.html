What is GIS and How Does It Work?

🌍 Geographic Information System

GIS is a powerful technology that combines maps with data to help you visualize, analyze, and understand geographic information. It creates smart maps that show relationships, patterns, and trends in your data.

🛰️ Satellite Imagery Overlay

Our app overlays official development plans directly onto Google satellite imagery, allowing you to see exactly how planned developments relate to the current landscape and existing structures.

📍 GPS Integration

Using your phone's GPS and survey numbers, the app provides precise location understanding, helping you identify exactly where you are standing in relation to development zones and plans.
