What is GIS and How Does It Work?

🌍 Geographic Information System

GIS is a powerful technology that combines maps with data to help you visualize, analyze, and understand geographic information. It creates smart maps that show relationships, patterns, and trends in your data.

🛰️ Satellite Imagery Overlay

Our apps overlay official development plans and custom maps directly onto Google satellite imagery, allowing you to see exactly how planned developments relate to the current landscape.

📍 GPS & Georeferencing

Using GPS integration and precision georeferencing tools, our apps provide accurate location understanding and allow you to transform any map into a georeferenced overlay.
