India Development Plan (DP) Maps – Online GIS Viewer

MapMagician is a free interactive GIS viewer for Development Plan, Layout Plan, Village and Regional maps across 40+ regions in India. Browse draft and final DP maps for Pune, Mumbai, Thane, Solapur, Nashik, Nagpur, Hyderabad HMDA periphery, Bengaluru, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar (Aurangabad), Kolhapur, Satara, Amravati and many more regions on an interactive satellite map with measurement tools. Currently covers Maharashtra, Telangana and Karnataka — expanding to new states and cities.

All regions are updated with the latest draft and notification maps released by state and city planning authorities including Town Planning departments, PMRDA, NAINA, MSRDC, HMDA, BBMP and more. Coverage expanding across Indian states.
