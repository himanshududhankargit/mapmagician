# dpplans-seo — notable sub-locations (SEO indexing map)

**Purpose.** Decide which sub-location pages get **indexed** vs left **noindex**. Policy: index the
region page + only **notable** sub-locations — real towns/cities or well-known pilgrimage / tourist /
industrial / educational hubs with genuine search demand for `<place> development plan` /
`<place> DP map` / `<place> land use`. The long tail of villages, city wards, hamlets and revenue
parcels stays **noindex** on purpose — indexing thin/duplicate pages at scale risks a site-wide
quality demotion (it does **not** save crawl budget; noindex pages are still crawled). Indexing the
~100 real towns below is upside with low rank risk.

**Coverage snapshot (2026-06-10).** ~2,560 sub-locations across 41 regions; **~135 already have
content** (indexed) from prior `seo-towns-wf` runs covering 31/41 regions. The genuinely indexable
*remaining* inventory is **~60–70 strong + ~40 borderline ≈ ~100 pages — not 2,500.**

**Vocabulary.** Maharashtra → "Development Plan" / "DP". Karnataka & Telangana → "Master Plan"
(Bengaluru: "RMP"). Reflect this in titles/keywords.

**Method.** 7 parallel research agents (one per geographic cluster) classified every uncovered
sub-location name (local geographic knowledge + web validation of population / municipal status /
published DP / real-estate or tourism demand). Tiny villages were excluded by design.

  Pushpak/Panvel) are not in its list.
- **dhule, nandurbar, aurangabad, nanded-waghala, jalna, hingoli** uncovered lists — only parent-city
  suburbs/hamlets; the notable district towns (Sillod, Kannad, Paithan; Deglur, Kinwat) are absent from
  the data and would need to be added upstream (menuGIS) to ever be indexable.

## Data hygiene

**Done** (`scripts/build-regions.js`, 2026-06-10): drop non-place labels (`RESERVED FOREST`,
`Protected forest`, `Tank Water Limit`, `Sea Sand, Mud & Rocks`, `GAP`, `URBAN`, `Top`, `Cantonment`,
`Core Area`) and `null`/blank rows; strip residual stray brackets from display names (`Kamothe)`,
`Khandala (M]`). Removed ~13 broken sub-pages.

**Remaining (do before/with the town-content run):**
- **Duplicates → thin near-dupe pages:** same town listed 2–6× (Ambajogai ×3, Tuljapur/Umarga/Bhum ×2,
  Khaparkheda ×4, Chicholi ×6, Kasani/Marathwadi/Vajegaon ×2 in mahabaleshwar). `attachVillageSlugs`
  disambiguates with `-2/-3` so the build doesn't break, but only ONE should be the indexed page —
  `dedupe-seo-gen.js` handles this for generated content; rely on it.
- **Self-region rows:** a village named like its own region (`Nasik`, `Nagar`, `Jalna`, `Nanded`,
  `Nandurbar`, `Latur`, `Bijapur`) — these are the region itself; keep noindex (don't promote).
- **Spelling normalization for any promoted town:** `Bhaindar`→Bhayandar, `Lonavale`→Lonavala,
  `KALYAN DOMBIVALI MUNCIPAL CORPORATION`→Kalyan-Dombivli, `Ichalakranji`→Ichalkaranji.
- **Mis-tags to verify before indexing:** a `Kolhapur` under palghar; a `Tasgaon` under kolhapur.
- **Cross-region duplicate villages:** naina & karjat corridors share ~40 identical village names.
