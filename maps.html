Development Plan (DP) Maps – Online GIS Viewer

MapMagician is an interactive GIS viewer for Development Plan and Master Plan maps across 40+ regions in India. Browse draft and final DP maps for Pune, Mumbai, Thane, Solapur, Nashik, Nagpur, Hyderabad HMDA periphery, Bengaluru, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar (Aurangabad), Kolhapur, Satara, Amravati and many more regions on an interactive satellite map with measurement tools. Peripheral village plans available. Currently covers Maharashtra, Telangana and Karnataka — expanding to new states and cities.

Coverage expanding across Indian states.
